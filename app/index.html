Don't let the simple look of this site fool you. Under the hood, it's doing something really neat.

A Service Worker is running to make this site work offline!

If you turn on airplane mode and refresh the page, you won't see the offline downasour. You'll remain connected to the site because a service worker is picking up the slack and serving the static files from your cache to you.
